Type of Sway

A sway is a stretch of one side of the body; towards or away from the moving leg. Used for balance, speed or initiation of movement, or aesthetics.
Moving in the opposite direction of the movement decreases speed and improves stability.
Moving in the direction of the movement increases speed and decreases stability.

Technical Sway
Entire body from foot to thead is stretched. Action can be perceived in the hip.
Releasing Sway / Broken Sway
Only the upper part of the body is involved. Position of the hip is not affected. One side is shortened and the other is stretched.
Cosmetic Sway
Shoulders and shoulder blades are stretched slightly without moving the head. The other side of the body is relaxed.

Extension

Stretch of the front of the upper body in relation to the back of the upper body.
The stretch starts at the head, neck, and chest and follows in the lower part abdomen (belly), and lumbar area (lower back).
